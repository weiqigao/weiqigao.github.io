Wednesday, June 14, 2018
Title: The New Java Release Cadence
Presented By: Paul Nauman
About the Presentation:

In this talk we'll examine Oracle's new release cadence for the Oracle JDK and the issues it creates. We'll also examine the relationship between Oracle JDK and OpenJDK, both now and in the projected future. We'll also touch on Azul's OpenJDK Support offerings, which can alleviate some of those impending problems.
About the Presenter:

Paul Nauman will update us on Oracle's new Java SE Release Cadence. Paul is a Sales Engineer for Azul Systems, helping Azul's customers validate Azul's products in their environments and maximize value from their use. Paul was previously a JVM engineer at Oracle and a Telecom Architect at Sun Microsystems before that. Paul has presented talks on JVM Internals at JavaOne and regularly attends several JUGs in his assigned territory. In his free time Paul enjoys reading philosophy and theology, and travelling with his family.
