Title: Kotlin for Android
Presented By: Bryan Thrall
About the Presentation:

Now that Kotlin is formally supported by Google, you may want to learn about how we are using it to produce a showcase Android app for WWT written entirely in Kotlin! What Kotlin features have we found useful? What problems have we stumbled over? How well does Kotlin work on Android? Well, I'll tell you!
About the Presenter:

Bryan Thrall is a software developer at WWT Asynchrony Labs and has worked for 14+ years in areas including mobile apps, counter-terrorism support, business data processing, and flight simulation.
