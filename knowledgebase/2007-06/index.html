Title: NetBeans Platform
Presented By: Tom Wheeler, OCI
About the Presentation:

In this presentation, Mr. Wheeler will explain problems that commonly plague large Swing-based applications and how the NetBeans platform can help you to solve them. He will describe the platform in detail, including its features, drawbacks and competitors and will also demonstrate how to create a complete platform-based application.
About the Presenters:

Tom Wheeler, Senior Software Engineer at OCI, has been working with the NetBeans platform since 2004. He won the NetBeans Community Award in 2006 for his participation in the open source project.
