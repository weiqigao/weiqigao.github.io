Support Resources

The JBoss Group is founded by Marc Fleury, also the founder of the JBoss project, to provide training and consulting support for JBoss. The forums on JBoss's website is answered by JBoss developers and contains lots of useful information.

The official mailing lists jboss-development and jboss-user are archived at Source Forge. GMANE offers a bidirectional nntp gateway to many mailing lists, including the JBoss lists.

The Core Developers Network is a newly formed company that provide training and consulting services for not only JBoss, but also other Open Source Java products.
