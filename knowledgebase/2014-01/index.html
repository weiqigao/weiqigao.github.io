Title: Agile Transformation
Presented By: Gint Grabauskas Savvis
About the Presentation:

Savvis, a CenturyLink company, has recently embarked on a journey to radically improve how projects are executed and ultimately delivered. An "agile transformation" is underway and showing early signs of big results. What began as a grass-roots movement has flourished into a fully executive sponsored initiative with game changing outcomes. Projects are coming in on-time, on budget, and quality has sky rocketed – all at a sustainable pace. This session will explore how agile was born at Savvis, critical lessons learned, and key take-aways to apply to your own agile transformation.
About the Presenter:

Gint Grabauskas works for Savvis, a CenturyLink Company as a Senior Director of Global Software Development. He is a servant leader and an agile coach who excels at building and maintaining high performing teams. Gint has come up through the ranks of software engineering via progressively more challenging roles including software developer, software architect, team lead, functional manager, and engineering director. He has a broad industry background including telecom, controls, enterprise software, healthcare, power, and most recently cloud computing. Gint is a transformative leader whose passion is the fusion of talent with software agility for world-class enterprise results. He genuinely believes that software development can be sustainable, humane, and fun – all the while delivering tremendous business value.
