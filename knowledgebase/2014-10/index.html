Title: Intro to Graph Databases with Neo4j
Presented By: Jacob Hansson
About the Presentation:

90% of humanity's stored information was created in the past two years, putting us in the middle of huge data storage problems - and opportunities. This talk will give you a technical introduction to the fastest growing segment of the database industry, Graph Databases, which specialize in storing, and asking questions to, highly complex and interconnected data. You'll learn the basics of graph databases, what use cases are in their sweet spot and the basics of the Cypher graph query language.

About the Presenter:

Jacob Hansson works for Neo Technology, the commercial backer of the open source Neo4j graph database, as the project lead for the Neo4j Server. He recently moved to Missouri and is setting up a small Neo4j office in Columbia.
