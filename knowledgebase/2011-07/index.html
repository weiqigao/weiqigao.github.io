Title: JWebSockets: On the road to WebSockets
Presented By: Brian Gilstrap (Riot Games)
About the Presentation:

HTML 5 and it's WebSockets spec are years away from being finalized, but developers are providing implementations like jWebSockets to gain their benefits before 2014. Come learn about WebSockets and the problems it solves, and explore using jWebSockets in your rich client applications. Along the way we'll look at previous and existing attempts to solve the server-side push problem and compare them to WebSockets.
About the Presenters:

Brian Gilstrap is a Senior Platform Engineer with Riot Games, where he works to create some of the best and most frequently played games in the world.
