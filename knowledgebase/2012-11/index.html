Title: Play Framework
Presented By: Randy Tielking
About the Presentation:

Play 2.0 application framework is the largest departure from J2EE since Spring and is inspired by some of the rapid development capabilities of Ruby on Rails and Django. This presentation will introduce Play, show how to develop with Play, and discuss deployment of Play applications.
About the Presenter:

Randy Tielking has been a software development consultant since 2002 working for large clients in St Louis and Minneapolis such as Express Scripts, the Federal Reserve Bank of St Louis, Carlson Companies, and Best Buy, His area of expertise is developing web based business applications using Java technologies such as Hibernate, JPA, Spring, JSF, and Richfaces.
