eXtreme Programming (XP)

By Brian Button, Object Mentor, Inc.

About the Presentation

In this presentation, you will learn the basics of Extreme Programming, including the underlying theory that make XP possible, the business case for it, and the principles and practices that describe it. You will also hear how three specific companies have used XP to their strategic advantage during the past year.

About Brian Button

Brian Button is a senior consultant at Object Mentor, Inc. In his current position, he travels the world teaching teams how to write better software through OO and the Extreme Programming practices.
