Title: Designing with Dependency Injection
Presented By: Alex Miller, MetaMatrix
About the Presentation:

Dependency Injection (DI) and inversion of control (IoC) have been hot topics lately due to the popularity of lightweight IoC containers such as Spring and Pico. In this presentation, we will examine the ways in which the use of dependency injection affects architecture and design. We will examine the positive and negative consequences of using DI as a design technique and how it affects configuration, testing, and maintainability. This talk focuses on the technique rather than any particular container.
About the Presenter:

Alex Miller has been working with Java and object-oriented technologies for 9+ years. For the past 5 years he has been working at MetaMatrix as head of the server team and Chief Architect of Data Integration. Alex holds a BS in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Champaign-Urbana and an MS in Computer Science from Washington University. Alex gave a presentation to the St. Louis JUG on JavaSpaces in 1999.
