Title: Git Happens
Presented By: Jessica Kerr
About the Presentation:

Distributed version control is here to stay. Even in Subversion climates, good developers use git locally. But what's the big deal? There’s a million tutorials on the internet that tell you what to type in Git, but not why! Learn how to use DVCS to tell the story of your project. You’ll get the point of all those extra steps in committing and updating; erase some Subversion-related preconceptions; and grasp the purpose of fast-forward merge, cherry-pick, and rebase. I promise: absolutely no demos. Instead, the concepts here will cause all the demos on the internet to magically make sense.
